Biography

I am a senior year PhD student at INRIA Sophia Antipolis, my supervisor is Francois Bremond. My main research topic is action recognition. I focus on recognition of daily-living actions in home environment. I am colaborating with Toyota robotic department to deploy proposed solutions on a robotic platform.

I obtained Master of Science Degree in 2009 at Pozna≈Ñ University of Technology, Faculty of Computing Science in Software Engineering. Between 2010-2012 I worked as a research engineer at Laboratory of Intelligent Decision Support Systems at Poznan University of Technology. In 2013 I did 6 months internship at INRIA STARS team.

From January until April 2016 I was an intern at Disney Research Pittsburgh. I was working on People Re-identification.
